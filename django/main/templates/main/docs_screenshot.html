How to take a screenshot.

Take a screenshot

  1. Click to open the left side menu.
    • Left side menu location
  2. Click the "Screenshot" icon on the top menu.
    • Screenshot Icon
  3. A screenshot will now be saved to your device as "screenshot.png".
    4. Screenshots are available for both user tiers.
    • Free users: Screenshots will contain a watermark.
    • Professional users: Screenshots will not contain a watermark.
